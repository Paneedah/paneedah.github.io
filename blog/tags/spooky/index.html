🎃 HALLOWEEN UPDATE & CUSTOM SPOOKINESS 🎃

The servers are going offline for Facepunch's Halloween update… but beware, our own terrors await when you log back in! 👻

Hey survivors! 👻

The time has come… Facepunch's annual Halloween update is here!
Our servers will be going offline shortly for a mandatory update and restart to bring in all the official Halloween goodness.

But that's not all—
When the lights come back on (or rather, when the fog rolls in 😈)… you'll find we've added our own horrifying twists to make this year's haunt extra deadly!

🎃 OUR HALLOWEEN ADDITIONS 🎃

💀 Fully Revamped Dungeons

Each level now features a unique spooky theme, with bosses boasting improved loot drops for those daring enough to descend into the darkness.

🎃 New Locked Crate Event – Radtown

A fresh event awaits: hack a locked crate while waves of flying pumpkin drones try to obliterate it (and you). Defend it if you can - the rewards are worth the fright!

🪦 Graveyards (Night Only)

When darkness falls, graveyards rise. Hunt the undead and loot the treasures they guard... if you survive till dawn.

👁️ Even More Spooky Surprises

We've tucked a few extra chills and thrills around the world—some sweet, some sinister. Keep your eyes peeled… and your weapon loaded.

🕯️ So grab your flashlights, polish your pumpkins, and prepare to scream.
The servers will be back soon—and when they are, Halloween truly begins. 🌕

See you on the other side… if you make it back. 💀
