Server Rules

⚠️ Important Notice

  • These rules apply both in-game and in Discord
  • Server management may adjust rules or take action as necessary to maintain a fair and enjoyable environment, even if not explicitly stated
  • Rust is a multiplayer game. If you make a choice that directly leads to the loss of your items/progress, the admin team will not be stepping in. We will only step in to reset and refund items/RP/XP that is lost through no fault of your own (e.g., encountering a bug)

Chat Rules​

🚫 Hate Speech/Racism

Hate speech and racism will not be tolerated. This includes but is not limited to:

❌Overtly hateful speech
❌Covertly hateful speech
❌Racism baiting
❌Chat filter work arounds
❌Offensive signs, fireworks, etc.
Penalty: Immediate ban

💬 Toxicity/Spamming/Excessive Trolling

Engaging in behavior that is intentionally toxic, annoying, and/or attention-seeking is not okay.

Penalty: Mute. Excessive/repeated offenses will result in a ban

⚖️ Arguing in Chat

Rust is, by nature, a toxic game. However, if arguments are continuously filling up the chat, the mod team will take action as appropriate for the situation.

🛡️ Arguing with Staff

We understand that things may happen that are frustrating; however, staff assistance is a privilege, not a right.

Remember: Be respectful — our team works hard to support the community. Disrespect may result in your ticket being closed without resolution, and can lead to a mute or further action

🎭 Impersonating Staff

Impersonating a member of staff will result in disciplinary action based on severity.

Penalty: Mutes to bans depending on severity

📢 Advertisement

Do not advertise other servers, products, or services.

💬 DMs and Staff Engagement

Do not DM staff members directly.

Instead: Create a support ticket if you have a problem

Server Rules​

⛔ Cheating

Using any form of cheats, hacks, or unauthorized third-party software.

Penalty: Immediate permanent ban

👥 Association

Knowingly playing with a cheater or benefiting from cheating.

Penalty: Minimum 30 day ban

🔒 Ban Evasion

Attempting to join the server while banned.

Not Allowed: You cannot play if you have a previous Server or Rust ban

🔨 Historical VAC/Game Bans

Recent bans on your Steam account may prevent you from joining.

Requirement: VAC/Game bans must be at least 30 days old

🐛 Exploits

Finding a bug is not a free pass to exploit it.

Action Required: Report bugs by creating a ticket. Abusing them will lead to bans

😈 Griefing

Intentionally disrupting, blocking, or annoying other players — often for the sake of attention, revenge, or personal satisfaction. This includes:

🚫Blocking bases or TC access
🚫Walling off monuments or essential areas
🚫Trapping players
🚫Stealing loot
🚫Using game mechanics to intentionally ruin another player's experience

📜 Questions About Rules?

If you're unsure about any rules or have questions, create a support ticket in our Discord. Don't risk a ban — ask first!
