Passive Prestige Perks

Notes & Tips

  • 🏃 You have to use /togglerun to gain your bonus running speed.
  • 🏊 You have to use /toggleswim to gain your bonus swimming speed.
  • 🌙 Rested EXP bonuses stack on top of base XP, this means you get more EXP for doing the same stuff.
  • 🛒 Shop discounts apply to all in-game purchases from the /s store (when bought from the server).
  • 🔓 Certain Prestiges unlock special abilities or quests, like Prestige 10 unlocking the Laginator quest.
  • ⚡ TC Upgrade multiplier affects how fast your Tool Cupboard takes to upgrade built structures.
  • 🎒 Backpack size increases with each prestige, or you can get more slots by Purchasing VIP!

All perks are automatically applied when you reach the corresponding Prestige level. You can prestige by using the /st command, once you reach level 100!

All prestige perks/benefits carry over when you prestige again! E.g. prestige from 1 -> 2, you keep the T1 techtree as well as unlocking T2!
