Leveling & XP Requirements

⚠️ Important Notes

  • XP listed is what is required to reach the next level.
  • You can prestige once you reach level 100 using the /st command.
  • Prestiging will reset your skilltree. Make sure to plan your builds before prestiging!
  • XP does not rollover when prestiging—start fresh from 0 XP.
  • Total XP for levels 0-100 is 10,273,693.
  • Skill points earned per level will reset upon prestiging.
  • Progression beyond level 100 is technically infinite, but XP requirements continue increasing.
