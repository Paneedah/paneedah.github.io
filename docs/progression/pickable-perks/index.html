Pickable Prestige Perks

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Once you pick a perk, you cannot change or undo it.
  • You keep your perks when you prestige again and can pick another one.

Pet Bear 🐻

Gives you a pet bear to command! Smash barrels, break trees, attack other animals, and ride with boosted movement speed. Your bear has its own inventory!

Teleport Gun 🔫

Gives you access to the Prestige Teleport Bolt. Shoot this rifle wherever you want on SOLID GROUND, and instantly teleport to the bullet's landing location (CAN CAUSE DEATH).

Casino 🎰

Allows you to purchase your own casino parts and run your casino! Be sure to place all gambling seats before the big wheel!

Terminal 🖥️

An indispensable item that manages house resources in one place. Placeable in-house, multifunctional for resource management or terminal-only factories.

Portable Recycler ♻️

Gain access to /recycler. Open your personal recycler anywhere, anytime.

Night Vision 🌙

Gain the /nv command. Set your personal time to 12 noon FOREVER. (Must be redone after swapping Worlds)

Double Jump 🏃‍♂️

Gain the ability to double jump. Yes, just like Mario!

Portable Locker 🗄️

Gain the /locker command. Quickly access your mining, woodcutting, and scientist kits on demand.

Predator Missile 🚀

Spawn your own Predator Missile to speed up raids or mess around with friends.

Helicopter Assist 🚁

Advanced helicopter controls including Hover Mode, Emergency Recovery, and intelligent auto-landing.

Floating Helicopter 🛶

Add flotation devices to your helicopter to land outside oil rigs and take off from water as an amphibious seaplane.
